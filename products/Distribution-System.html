DISTRIBUTION SYSTEM


Distribution systems are used for uniform collection of the fed water owing to its larger area of collection. Astox offers quality range of distribution systems for all types of filters, softeners and demineralization plants.

Features:

  • Compatible with all FRP pressure vessels whether threaded or flange.
  • Easy to install.
  • Ensure uniform distribution and collection.
  • Corrosion resistant.
